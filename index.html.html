Perks and cons of having a dog

Having a pet especially dogs can be very stressful [especially puppies] but these adorable bundle of joy keeps us going everyday.Here we are going to teach you how to nuture your best friends.

First lets talk about feeding.It has been advised that you should not feed your dog more than 15% of their weight per meal in order to keep them healthy.Puppies shoul be feed with milk[hand feed or natural weaning] and/or wet meal.They should not be introduced to dry meal or meat till they are over six months old.adult can be feed with dry meal but with a nearby water source,meat can also be added but not raw.There are other alternatives like processed meals and pallets but they should all be used in moderation

Grooming

When bathing or grooming ur pet for the first time remember to use a leash, water coming from a hose or its hair being cut with a scissors is unnatural so do your best to calm it down.After the excersice remember to give them a treat.Before bathing a dog ensure youe excersise them and make them nice and ready so cooling off in the water will not be a bad idea.On the other hand when grooming ur dog you need to keep them as calm as possible in order not to wound them with the grooming instrument.You should carefully choose your soap brand based on the size and breed of your dog.Some of these popular brands include

Playtime is a very important time for dogs especially puppies.This is a time to bond with their owners/parents and themselves.This is also a time to let of steam and calm down.Some dogs enjoy playtime more than others due to factors like breed,age,equipment availability
